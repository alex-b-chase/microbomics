Research focus

Understanding microbiomes through traits, interactions, and environmental context.

01

Eco-evolutionary dynamics

We study how microbial populations diversify across environmental gradients, linking fine-scale genetic variation to ecological function.

02

Chemical traits & interactions

We investigate specialized metabolites and biosynthetic gene clusters as functional traits that mediate microbial interactions.

03

Microbiomes & biogeochemistry

We connect microbial community structure to carbon cycling, methane oxidation, and environmental change across soils and marine sediments.
